Alfredo Sauce

Description

This alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the butter. Then stir the cream and simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cheese and garlic until heated through and smooth.
  3. Stir in the parsley.
