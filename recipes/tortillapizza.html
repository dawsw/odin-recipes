Breakfast Tortilla Pizza/h1>

Description

This hearty breakfast tortilla pizza could not be easier!

Ingredients

  • 11/2 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 1 tortilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup total assorted toppings, such as cheese, hot sauce, cooked meat or veggies

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Rub butter along the bottom and sides of a cake pan. Press tortilla into the pan and crack each egg next to each other.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and add desired toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven until egg whites are set and tortilla has become lightly browned.
